The basics of Equilibreism

Ideologies:

Non-monetary

Equilibreism is a non-monetary system, meaning there is no primary currency, instead goods are provided in return for work. Consumer goods are buoght with a currency however this currency can only be used for consumer grade goods

Basic needs

Any person who works, no matter their jobs will have their basics needs provided for them

Meritism

Wages are given based off of contribution to society, not off heirarchical position

Decentralized

Distribution of goods is completely decentralized. There is still however a central government.

Classless

An Equilibreist society isa classless one, the wage gap between citizens will be kept low.
